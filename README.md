## üë• Who is ClickSync for?

ClickSync is for you if:

- You watch TV Shows & Movies online with friends.
- Your friends join a call (on Discord, Google Meet, etc.) and one person streams the media.

It allows each person to play/pause, rewind/forward, and control the volume of the video.
